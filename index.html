06  ·  Challenge #1

Procrastination

Delayed Execution

Procrastination is not laziness — it is a misalignment between intention and action. Left unaddressed, it compounds into missed deadlines, last-minute cramming, and academic underperformance.

High Impact Risk
  • ⏰ Time Deferral — Tasks pushed to the last moment, reducing quality of output.
  • 📉 Consistency Erosion — Irregular study habits lead to fragmented knowledge retention.
  • 😰 Stress Spiral — Accumulated work creates anxiety that further reduces performance.
  • 🔁 Cycle Reinforcement — Without intervention, the pattern repeats each semester.

07  ·  Challenge #2

Perfectionism

The Overthinking Trap

Perfectionism masquerades as a strength but operates as a constraint. The pursuit of flawless output often results in paralysis — no submission beats a submitted draft.

Medium-High Risk
  • 🔍 Overthinking — Excessive analysis before starting leads to wasted planning time.
  • 🚫 Reduced Output — Fear of imperfection prevents submission of adequate work.
  • 🧠 Mental Bandwidth Drain — Energy spent perfecting minor details depletes focus.
  • 📊 Productivity Deficit — Fewer completed tasks despite high intellectual investment.
